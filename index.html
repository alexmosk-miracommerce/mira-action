We stand with Ukraine

Get involved in Mira Action

In response to the escalated crisis in Ukraine, Mira Commerce has launched Mira Action, a non-profit organization to provide humanitarian aid and medical care to those affected by the war in Ukraine. Since February 24, 2022, our team of volunteers has been able to assist refugees in countless ways and we are thankful to hundreds of generous donors.

Mira Action team purchases, outfits, and delivers emergency response vehicles and ambulances to rural areas in Ukraine. We source, transport, and distribute food, medications, baby products, power generators, blankets, and many other supplies from Poland into Ukraine. On our return trips from the war zone, we help evacuate a growing number of refugees from Ukraine.

A note from our president:

Dear friends, partners, and our wider eCommerce industry members:

The war in Ukraine is a travesty and a terrible tragedy for mankind, it affects us all. Our parent agency, Mira Commerce, has been directly impacted - over 20 of our associates and their families, including that of my own, had to shelter from bombs and rockets, to evacuate, to flee from hot war zones, to survive inhumane conditions before getting to safety. We are fortunate to have been able to evacuate and relocate all of our people. But many thousands of others did not make it, and before the war is over, we consider it our duty to put a maximum effort into saving lives and helping those who are greatly suffering from war.

Amid the darkest of times, we have seen an unwavering amount of humanity, unity, and love. Being on the ground in Poland and helping with humanitarian relief efforts, I have experienced this firsthand. Strangers approach us with fuel, supplies, and offer their residences as refugee shelters. Seeing countless lines of women and children approaching the border with their strollers through freezing rain and snow, border guards run to them and wrap shivering people in blankets, cover them with their arms and say “you are safe now” with tears on their faces. I know my life has forever been changed seeing this side of us, seeing us all as ONE. We may identify ourselves as entrepreneurs, business owners, managers, mothers, fathers, wives, husbands, daughters, sons, but we are all human. And as human living souls, we love, we build, we create, and now we must work together to overcome this catastrophe and not allow tragic evil history to repeat itself ever again.

The outpouring of support we have received from our clients, partners, competitors, and industry patrons offering financial assistance, logistical help, and volunteering efforts has left us speechless. Each donation has directly added an extra layer of warmth, fed an extra mouth, eased someone’s suffering, or saved a life. I am honored to associate with individuals like yourselves, with like minded souls that help make this a better world. If we can help one person at a time, one step at a time, one action at a time – the wave of change will be massive.

I invite you to join our efforts in funding and growing Mira Action as a non-partisan non-profit organization focused on direct humanitarian assistance to people who need it the most. Thank you for standing with us, standing with Ukraine, standing united. Time for us to act together!

God Bless,

Sergei Ostapenko, President
